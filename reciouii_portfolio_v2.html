How I work

The process

01
Brief & diagnosis
You share your footage, goals, and any reference. I analyze what the content needs — pacing, hook structure, energy. I flag issues before I start cutting so there are no surprises at delivery. You know what you're getting before I touch the timeline.
02
Engineering the edit
Every cut is a decision. I build from the hook backward — what does the viewer need to feel in second 0–3 to stay until the end? After Effects motion where it earns its place. Audio design that supports emotion. Color grading that matches your brand identity.
03
Delivery in 48 hours
You receive the final edit within 48 hours via Google Drive or WeTransfer. One revision round included. I also send a brief note explaining why specific decisions were made — so you understand the strategy, not just the output.
04
Results tracking (retainer clients)
For monthly clients: I review what performed, what didn't, and why — every month. The next batch of Reels is built on data from the last batch. This is how content improves instead of staying flat.
